About us

Hi and welcome to Youvelo.com

We are a first of its kind website, connecting bike rental companies with clients from all over the world. Our site will easily allow a bike rental store to setup and customise a profile so that they can reach customers from around the world. YouVelo will take care of booking, scheduling, sizing, payment and advertising so you don’t have to, allowing you to focus on delivering the best service to your clients.

On the client side, it will allow a user to find a bike store that delivers exactly what they need, whether that be a premium carbon frame to tackle Alpe d’Huez or a hybrid bike to take the family around the Burgundy countryside, they will quickly be able to find what is the most relevant to them. Sign-up below, come be part of the largest network for bike rental in Europe and join us on this exciting adventure.
